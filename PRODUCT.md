# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Designers and project teams** managing interior design workflows. They work across multiple project stages: measurements, 3D design, revisions, and production drawings. Users need clarity on project status, task ownership, and timeline progression without cognitive overload.

## Product Purpose

A collaborative project management tool for interior design studios. It simplifies the journey from client brief to finished design by providing clear role-based workflows, real-time status tracking, and a beautiful interface that inspires rather than bureaucratizes the creative process. Success is measured by adoption among creative professionals who value both craft and operational clarity.

## Brand Personality

Professional-reliable, with a warmth that avoids cold minimalism. The tool should feel like a trusted creative partner, not a corporate system. Elegant, purposeful, inspired—clarity without sterility.

## Anti-references

- Generic corporate SaaS (cold, flat, overly austere)
- Overly trendy interfaces that age poorly
- Cluttered or overwhelming dashboards
- Minimalism that sacrifices warmth or approachability
- Designs that hide complexity rather than taming it

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity through hierarchy**, not complexity. Information architecture should guide the eye without requiring multiple steps to understand status or next actions.
2. **Inspired, not bureaucratic.** The interface serves the creative process; it should feel like a tool for creatives, not a compliance form.
3. **Warm professionalism.** Professional reliability paired with human warmth. Use color and typography to convey approachability, not formality.
4. **Purposeful motion and detail.** Every animation, micro-interaction, and visual accent should enhance understanding or delight—never gratuitous.
5. **Role-aware design.** Different users (Designer, Coordinator, Marketer, Admin) see tailored workflows. Design should make their specific job immediately obvious.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG AA compliance** (minimum)
- Full support for `prefers-reduced-motion` (all animations must have non-motion alternatives)
- Adequate color contrast: body text ≥4.5:1, placeholder text ≥4.5:1
- Keyboard navigation fully functional
- Screen reader support for all interactive elements and role-specific workflows
